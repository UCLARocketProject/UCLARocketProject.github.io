Cerberus 2015-2016

Overview

Cerberus continues to use the M1575 hybrid motor but includes three parrallel staged solid motors to support its bigger size. Known as Take-Off Assist Motors (TAMS), they currently do not seperate after launch, but development is continuing in this direction. The payload is also expanded and features a deployable glider with onboard electronics. Recovery has been refined and tested for reliability. Major work was also done to overhaul the ignition system to support new demands. Cerberus flew successfully at IREC to just over 6300 ft.
