About URP

UCLA Rocket Project is the biggest rocket engineering project at UCLA. The team is composed of students majoring in various disciplines, such as Mechanical/Aerospace Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Physics, Computer Science, and many others. Each year, teams compete at the Intercollegiate Rocket Engineering Competition where they must launch and successfully recover a 10lb payload to either 10,000 or 25,000 ft above ground level. Last year UCLA Rocket Project had its first successful launch and recovery with a hybrid motor in the past 5 years. This year, our major works involve the following: developing a scientific payload, improving on the rocket to perform better in competition, and beginning the 2-year production of our custom hybrid engine. Join us on our journey to build awesome rockets!
