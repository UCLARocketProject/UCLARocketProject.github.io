Looking to Get Involved?

All UCLA students are welcome to attend our meetings. No prior knowledge is necessary. Our Rocket Lab is located in the Student Creativity Center on the second floor of Boelter Hall. The easiest way to find the lab is to take the stairs between Boelter Hall and CNSI. From that direction, we are the second door past the double doors which enter into Boelter Hall second floor. When we are in the lab, we will make sure to prop the door open for easy access. There is a sign for AIAA and Rocket Project right outside the door. If you have a passion for space or engineering, please drop by!
