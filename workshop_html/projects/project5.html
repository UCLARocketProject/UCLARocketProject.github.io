Flight Model

Welcome to the world where soaring usually means accessing memory out of bounds

Who doesn't like to fly! --------- Programmers. That was easy.

In all seriousness judgements about combination of interests, such as between programming and flying are in bad taste. Especially considering this website is being written by a philosopher-programmer. It was just a joke, OK?

The idea behind a flight model is the ability to predict the rocket's path based on incomplete data. That includes both a break in communication, after receiving some data about flight and low refresh speed of the live data. Apart from interpolation, a live-update flight model could tell us things about current predicted apogee and predicted landing zone.

There's a lot of math involved and learning, so most of the things your parents warned you about... Or at least ours did. If you're interested in making reality on a computer, then you're either a digital God or passionate the same things we are.
