UCLA Rocket Project Electronics Workshop Home

Welcome to the main page of the Electronics Workshop by Rocket Project at UCLA. Not sure why we're using Rocket Project at UCLA and UCLA Rocket Project? Neither are we, that's why we're using both. Just to be safe.

The purpose of the website (as if everything had a purpose) is to provide easier access to educational materials provided during the workshops, allowing for revision and hopefully covering the material from scratch.

Location and Time

Workshops take place in the SCC lab (the Rocket Project Lab) every Friday at 4:00 pm.

In order to reach the SCC lab, please take the second door to your as you're descending from the south-eastern outdoor stairs of Boelter.

Lessons

The following are the currently projected lessons (in order, temporal, not alphabetical):

Week 2, Fall 2016 Foundations of Digital Electronics
Week 3, Fall 2016 Introduction to AVR Programming
Week 4, Fall 2016 KiCad - Electrical Design using a computer
Week 5, Fall 2016 Soldering - Theory and Practise
Week 6, Fall 2016 Arduino Libraries - Much good, Much Unavoidable
Week 7, Fall 2016 Unix programming - Socks programming (short for Socket)
