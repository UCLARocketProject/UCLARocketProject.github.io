Workshop 2 - Introduction to AVR Programming

The second workshop will cover the basics of AVR programming, but most of it will apply to all electronics related programming, not matter what hardware you end up using.

And to reiterate, Atmel did not pay us. Although we're really hoping that they will... For now let's just say that we really do believe that they are the best for our applications.

Preparation for the workshop: We'll be working with the Arduino IDE, tough without using its capabilites yet, so please bring your computer with the IDE installed. Actually on second thought we'll be working with our heads more than with the Arduino IDE, so please, most of all bring your head.
